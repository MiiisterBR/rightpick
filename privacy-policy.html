Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

RightPick ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

RightPick collects and processes the following information:

  • Product Information: When you visit supported websites (Digikala, Torob, Esam), the extension reads product titles to search for price comparisons.
  • User Preferences: Language settings and OpenAI API key (if provided) are stored locally in your browser.

How We Use Information

  • Product titles are sent to Torob and Esam APIs to fetch price comparisons.
  • If you provide an OpenAI API key, product names may be sent to OpenAI for intelligent product matching.
  • All preferences are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers.

Data Storage

  • All user settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • We do not have any servers or databases that store your personal data.
  • Your OpenAI API key is stored locally and only sent directly to OpenAI's servers.

Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Torob API: To fetch product prices from torob.com
  • Esam API: To fetch product prices from esam.ir
  • Digikala API: To fetch product reviews from digikala.com
  • OpenAI API (optional): For AI-powered product matching if you provide an API key

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Product search queries are only sent to the APIs mentioned above for the sole purpose of providing price comparisons.

Your Rights

  • You can clear all stored data by removing the extension.
  • You can delete your OpenAI API key at any time from the extension settings.
  • You have full control over when the extension is active.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
